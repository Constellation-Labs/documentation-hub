## ◽ Quick Start Guides

Welcome to the Constellation Network validator node <b>QUICK START</b> guide!

The documentation here will guide you through the steps to build your node in a <b>quick</b> 
fashion without extensive explanations. 

*Please refer to the normal [installation walk-throughs](/validate/setup-guides/) for detailed step-by-step instructions with explanations.*

## ◽ Prerequisites

Before you begin you should verify all prerequisites are met to ensure you have everything properly in place to minimize disruptions in your work flow, troubleshotting, or delays.

Review the prerequisites [here](/validate/quick-start/prerequisites).
